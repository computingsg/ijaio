International Junior Artificial Intelligence Olympiad

Pilot for Singapore schools (19 Jan 2026 - 15 Feb 2026)

Open to all secondary schools, junior colleges / centralised institute, ITE and polytechnic students (including those awaiting admission)

International Junior Artificial Intelligence Olympiad (IJAIO) is the rebranding of the previous National Junior Artificial Intelligence Olympiad (NJAIO) as it:

  • better reflects the international scope of the event which spans over 50 countries as at 2026
  • distinguishes itself from National Olympiads in Artificial Intelligence (NOAI) which caters to only high school students and is typically highly selective to a minority group

IJAIO aspires to be a more inclusive and accessible platform for all students (from primary to post-secondary) embarking on their AI learning journey.

From the Singapore pilot run, we have been learning many valuable lessons to help us further refine and improve the outreach for the inaugural IJAIO in August 2026. Students who have participated in the pilot run will receive prioritised learning content and free access to the actual IJAIO August 2026 event. We look forward to your active participation and useful feedback!
